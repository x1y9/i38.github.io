What information we collect

You can access Battery Guard anonymously, and Battery Guard does not collect any personal information. Battery Guard is a battery information viewer and battery life optimizer, so it requires a number of permissions, which are explained below.

  1. Foreground service: Battery Guard detects power in the background, in order to prevent the background from being cleaned by the system, so you need to apply for foreground service permission.
  2. Network access: Battery Guard only requires network verification when activated, but not network access at other times.
  3. Vibrate access: Battery Guard needs vibrate permission when prompting the user's battery level.
